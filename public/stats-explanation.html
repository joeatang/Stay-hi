üü¢ Active Now
What it means: Users who have been active in the last 24 hours.
Data source: Count of unique users with recent session activity.
Updates: Refreshes every few minutes, fluctuates throughout the day.
Technical: SELECT COUNT(DISTINCT user_id) FROM auth.sessions WHERE updated_at > NOW() - INTERVAL '24 hours'
üí¨ Today's Hi's
What it means: Total number of "Hi" interactions sent today (Hi moments, messages, waves).
Data source: Cumulative count of all Hi-related actions for current day.
Updates: Increases throughout the day, resets at midnight.
Technical: Incremented by increment_total_hi() RPC when users interact
ü§ù Total Connections

Updates: Increases every time someone sends a Hi Wave, never resets.

Technical: Incremented by increment_hi_wave() RPC when users send Hi Waves
