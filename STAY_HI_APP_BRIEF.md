## FAQ (5 Q&As)

**Q: Is this a journaling app?**  
A: Not exactly. It's lighter. Think of it as a daily check-in tool with a memory. You can write as much or as little as you want. Some days it's just two emojis. That counts.

**Q: Do I have to share my moments publicly?**  
A: Nope. Everything is private by default. You choose what to share, when, and whether it's anonymous or public. Most people keep 80% of their moments private.

**Q: What if I miss a day?**  
A: Nothing happens. No guilt trips. No "you broke your streak" shame. You just pick up whenever you're ready. Consistency matters, but so does being human.

**Q: How is this different from Instagram or BeReal?**  
A: No comments. No likes. No DMs. No endless scrolling. No algorithm deciding what you see. It's built for reflection, not performance. You can share if you want, but the app works perfectly if you never do.

**Q: Who made this?**  
A: Joe Atang (Help Inspyre / Hi). It started as a personal tool to stay grounded during transitions, and became something worth sharing. It's designed by someone who actually uses it every day.
